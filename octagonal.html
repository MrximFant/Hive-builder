Strategic Overview

This canvas represents the dynamic world of our builder project. Each colored cluster signifies an active alliance, developing its territory and resources. The central brown structure is the main 'City', a hub of activity. Observe how different alliances establish their presence with key structures like Bases and Furnaces.

The 'Orange' Hive Initiative

The 'Orange' alliance demonstrates our primary expansion strategy. Currently in a foundational phase, this "hive" is designed for progressive outward growth, securing new territories and resources. Its development showcases the core principles of establishing a resilient and productive presence.

Key characteristics: Modular expansion, resource efficiency, strategic placement.

Key Structures & Components

Alliances utilize several core structures to build and sustain their territories:

Furnace (5x5 Red Tiles)

The powerhouse of an alliance. Furnaces are crucial for processing resources and fueling advanced construction.

Base (3x3 Tiles - often Grey/Dark)

The command and control center for an alliance's operations within a sector. Essential for expansion and defense.

Other Alliances

Various other alliances (purple, blue, yellow, etc.) employ similar strategies, establishing their own networks of Bases and Furnaces.
